A Letter From Our Lead Pastor

"Who am I, Sovereign LORD, and what is my family, that You have brought me this far?" 2 Samuel 7 : 18

My Beloved Friends,

Over a decade ago, when I accepted the sacred calling to serve as Lead Pastor of Shepherd's House Worship Center, I found myself echoing the words of King David. Today, as I reflect on this journey, those same words rise again from a heart overwhelmed by the faithfulness of God.

We have witnessed both victories and trials, moments of celebration and seasons of stretching. Yet through it all, one truth has remained unshaken: Christ, the Head of the Church, has been faithful.

He has guided us, strengthened us, and empowered us to carry His message beyond walls, into every place He has positioned us, especially within our communities of Charlottesville and Lynchburg. We also pause in gratitude for the many vessels God has used to bring the vision of "Equipping to Equip" into living reality.

With love, gratitude, and expectation, Rev. Dr. Kwame Akuamoah-Boateng Lead Pastor, Shepherd's House Worship Center
