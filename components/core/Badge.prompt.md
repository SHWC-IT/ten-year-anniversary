A tracked-caps pill for dates, times, and status tags.

```jsx
<Badge>October 9 - 11</Badge>
<Badge variant="outline">9:00 PM ET</Badge>
<Badge variant="muted">Coming Soon</Badge>
```

Variants: `solid` (gold fill) · `outline` (gold hairline) · `muted` (neutral). Drop an inline SVG icon as the first child for time/venue chips.
