The Next Decade

The Prayer Tabernacle.

As we enter our next decade, God has placed a clear mandate on our Lead Pastor's heart: to establish a Prayer Tabernacle, a consecrated place where seekers from all walks of life can encounter the presence of God, tarry in prayer, and behold His glory in Spirit and in Truth.

To begin this sacred work, we are prayerfully seeking to raise over $100,000. We humbly invite you to stand with us as a Kingdom partner, sowing into a vision that will impact generations and create a place where lives are transformed through divine encounter.
